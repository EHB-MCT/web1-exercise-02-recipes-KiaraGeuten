Ingredienten

  • Chex Cereal (12oz box)
  • Pretzel Sticks (160z bag)
  • Minature Marshmallows (10oz bag)
  • M&M’s Fall (11.40oz bag)
  • Candy Corn (18.5oz bag)
  • Raisins (12oz box)

Bereiding

  1. The directions are very simple: mix all ingredients together. (I did not add the whole box of raisins or whole bag of pretzels.) That’s it…super easy! The snack mix is also versatile. You can easily adjust ingredients and amounts.
